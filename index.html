How it all Started?

Pramey Jain and Meet Semlani - Both co-founders of Tartan & Batik have worked together for many years and that’s how they were driven by a common goal of helping the gig economy.


And that’s how the Tartan product was built in June 2021. At Tartan, they focus on real-time data and verification (through a single API), enabling consent-driven income, employment, and identity verification in less than a minute. And this unique solution is built via direct sources like payrolls, govt APIs, etc.

They didn’t stop there. Birth of another big idea:

With 6 months into building Tartan, Meet and Pramey realized there is another unsaid, unattended need in the market - The employees. While the larger problems for companies were addressed, no one was providing solutions for employees or workers. That’s how the Batik distribution platform began.

Our partnership has grown to over 60+ brand partners and 120+ products listed on our platform (and growing). We empower over one million employees to have immediate access and personalized benefits that address their unique needs and help them achieve a stress-free life.

With our unified employee benefits suite, we provide you with the best of healthcare, financial wellbeing, insurance, fitness benefits, and discounts that support talent acquisition, retention, and growth.
